AI for Property Management

Automate the busywork across leasing, maintenance, and resident communication — without replacing the systems you already run.

Book a strategy call See how we work

Property management runs on a flood of inbound requests, renewals, and vendor coordination — most of it handled by hand across email, phone, and your PMS. We put AI on the repetitive parts so your team spends its time on the exceptions that actually need judgment.

The problem

What the day looks like before AI does the repetitive work.

  • Maintenance and leasing requests handled manually across email, phone, and portals.
  • Slow first responses that frustrate residents and owners.
  • Institutional knowledge trapped in inboxes and individual staff.

Where AI helps in property management

High-leverage use cases we deploy — recommended off-the-shelf where a great tool exists, custom where it doesn't.

Maintenance triage & routing

Classify every request, gather missing details, and dispatch to the right vendor or team automatically.

Leasing assistant

Answer prospect questions, qualify leads, and schedule tours around the clock.

Resident communications

Draft and send renewal notices, rent reminders, and updates in your voice.

Owner reporting

Turn portfolio data into clear, on-demand owner updates.

Knowledge copilot

Answer staff questions from your leases, SOPs, and policies.

How we deliver
